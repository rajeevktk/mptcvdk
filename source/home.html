EK Nayanar Memorial Model Polytechnic College, Kallyassery Established in 1997 by the Govt. of Kerala, E.K Nayanar Memorial Model Polytechnic College, Kallyassery is a new generation Polytechnic offering courses in emerging technologies. The institution is managed by Institute of Human Resources Development (IHRD) fully owned and controlled by Govt. of Kerala. College is located in NH-17 midway between Kannur and Taliparamba towns. This institution has a proven track record of academic excellence among the other polytechnic colleges in Kerala. Admission is fully based on merit and as per the Govt. rules. E. K. Nayanar Memorial Model Polytechnic College is affiliated to Board of Technical Education, Kerala and approved by Government of kerala.
