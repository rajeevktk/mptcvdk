A diploma in computer engineering or computer science engineering is a 10+3 level of academic programme. It provides students with the basic knowledge of computer science, mathematics and computing techniques and engineering concepts. Students enrolled in the course acquire skills that help them to become an IT professional and enter the world of work. The duration of the course is three years. The course is composed of core concepts in computer science, such as operating systems, databases, and mobile computing. It also includes communication skills. Its curriculum is designed to prepare students for various job roles. Students can opt to pursue their careers as software developers or go on to pursue higher education. A diploma in computer engineering will equip you with the skills to program in a particular language and make you employable in the field. The diploma you earn may be in Java, Android, or web programming, which you can specialize in. The coursework for a diploma in computer engineering will cover a variety of software and hardware topics. You will learn how to code in Java and C++, as well as the theory of programming languages. You'll also learn how to evaluate algorithms and design computer-based systems.
Objectives:
* To provide every student with a strong conceptual foundation in the core areas of their subject.
* To enable students to engineer ingenious solutions with the greatest degree of accuracy, flexibility and based on the latest engineering paradigms.
* To familiarize students with the latest in Electronics Engineering
* To provide the greatest amount of pragmatism in approaching an engineering solution.
* To provide students with hands-on engineering experience through laboratory courses and project.
* To coach students to work in teams and provide insight and direction into the atmosphere as might be encountered by professionals in the field.
