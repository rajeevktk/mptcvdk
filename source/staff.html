Staff Club

    Staff Club is a cultural forum of the teaching staff and non-teaching staff of the polytechnic. It aims to facilitate positive interaction between the staff of the college. The club arranges farewell functions to teaching and non-teaching staff at the end of the academic year . It organizes one staff tour every year. The club is very active in all the general developmental activities of the polytechnic. Every year elected representatives of the staff members form the executive committee a President and a Secretary. Various common requirements are met by the staff club.
