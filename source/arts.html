Arts & Sports Club

ARTS CLUB:
    Arts club of the polytechnic provides an opportunity to the students to let their imagination run wild and provides them with the sight to view things in a different way. Students learn from one another and share their prowess in different aspects of art. The polytechnic provides ample opportunities to develop talents in different forms of art like music, singing, dance, drama etc. In addition to the activities of the Arts club, students are encouraged to take part in competitions held outside the campus.

Objectives:

  1. The main objective of Arts Club is to encourage the students in various arts activities.
  2. To improve the imagination skill in Entertainment.
  3. To help the students develop their leadership abilities.

SPORTS CLUB:
    Sports Club Activities play a vital role that can change a student’s life incredibly for ever. Today students’ involvement in Sports on Campus is inevitable for highly potential benefits. Sports Clubs exist for the general interest in physical activity through voluntary competition, formal competition, instruction, participation, or performance by students. Through sports activities, students are making their lives as pleasant as they like as well as they are moving ahead in their future career in sports and out of sports.

Objectives:

  1. To encourage and motivate students to involve in various sports activities at both inter and intra college level
  2. It helps in developing positive attitude, self-confidence, courage & patience.
  3. It helps in developing his / her overall skill set.
  4. Gets the opportunity to meet the people from different walks of life.
